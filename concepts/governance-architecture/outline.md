# Governance Architecture - Outline

*Draft v0.1*

## Core Thesis

Government institutions fail not because of bad people, but because of bad architecture. Like buildings, institutions have structural elements that determine their functionâ€”and structural flaws that predict their failures.

## Main Argument

1. **Institutions are designed systems** (not natural phenomena)
2. **Design choices have consequences** (predictable failure modes)
3. **Architecture matters more than personnel** (structure shapes behavior)
4. **Most institutional failures are architectural** (not moral)
