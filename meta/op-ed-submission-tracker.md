# Op-Ed Submission Tracker

**Purpose**: Track where each op-ed has been submitted and current status
**How to Use**: Update status as you submit, receive responses, or get feedback

---

## Status Key

- **Not Submitted** - Ready but not yet sent
- **Submitted** - Sent to outlet, awaiting response
- **Follow-up Sent** - Sent follow-up email after 2 weeks
- **Under Review** - Editor confirmed they're reviewing
- **Accepted** - Approved for publication
- **Published** - Live on outlet
- **Rejected** - Declined (note feedback if provided)
- **Withdrawn** - Pulled from consideration
